ABOUT

Women in Computer Science (WiCS) at Stony Brook University is proud to present its 7th annual hackathon, now held in a hybrid environment. HopperHacks is a 12-hour social good-themed hackathon that welcomes all students and is devoted to being inclusive and beginner-friendly.

Regardless of major or gender, we welcome you to join us in making an impact in the community with your creativity and imagination! We aim to provide a safe and comfortable environment for undergraduate and graduate students of any background and skill level to participate in.

Create projects that can inspire and create change toward problems in our world. Get involved with HopperHacks by participating as a Hacker! To register you must fill out this form.

Stony Brook University students are welcome to hack in person at the Stony Brook Union Ballroom, where you will be able to collaborate with one another face to face and attend some in-person activities. Food will also be provided.

If you would like to hack virtually, you can do that too! Mentors, volunteers, and attendees will all be on Discord, and judging and most workshops will also be virtual. Details are subject to change based on the state of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

If you have registered and are no longer able to attend, please let us know by emailing us at: wics@cs.stonybrook.edu

We are currently seeking volunteers to help hackers at HopperHacks 2022! Volunteers will help food setup, monitor chat, assist workshops, etc.

All times mentioned in this for is in EST. To volunteer you must fill out this form.

Volunteer Positions:

  • Registration: Sign in registered attendees
  • Activity Helpers: Monitor the workshop/activity and assist workshop/activity teacher if needed
  • Discord Monitors: Help attendees, company reps on Discord by answering their questions and directing them to channels
  • Judge Helpers: Direct judges to the appropriate Discord channels


*Note: You do not need to be present for the entire duration of the event*

We are currently seeking mentors to help hackers at HopperHacks 2022! Mentors will be available to help with coding questions and hackers' projects during the day of the hackathon. This involves being active on discord or being present in person to help anyone who needs it.

To mentor you must fill out this form.

*Note: You do not need to be present for the entire duration of the event*
